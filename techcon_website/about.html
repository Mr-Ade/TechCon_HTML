Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and has since grown into a globally recognized technology conference. What started in a modest conference hall now attracts over 5,000 attendees from around the world.

Over the years, we've been at the forefront of technological innovation, providing a platform for groundbreaking ideas and connecting the brightest minds in the tech industry.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing in the technology sector. Our core mission is to:

  • Promote cutting-edge technological advancements
  • Connect industry leaders, innovators, and emerging talent
  • Provide a platform for meaningful discussions on tech's future
  • Inspire the next generation of technological pioneers
